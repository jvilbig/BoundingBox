Image labelling app

Step 1

Open cone-labelling.herokuapp.com/label. You will see an image containing a telephone pole, the image ID and a Submit button.

Step 2

Zoom in the page and carefully drag-and-drop a tight bounding box around the telephone pole. You can remove previous boxes and redo if needed by clicking the X on the top right corner of the box.

Step 3

Click submit!

Attention!

Some images contain more than one pole, please pay attention to the background and label all of them.

Problems?

If something seems wrong (missing pole in image or labeling issues), send us a report including the image ID.
